Who I am

A Voice
on Every Peak

Sangat Thapa is a vocalist, adventurer, and storyteller rooted in the heart of Nepal. As the lead voice of the band Symmetry, he channels raw emotion into every performance — weaving melodies that resonate long after the last note fades.

Beyond the stage, Sangat finds his rhythm in the mountains. An avid trekker, he has walked some of Nepal's most breathtaking trails and leads others on those same journeys as Trek & Marketing Director at Kairos Tours and Travel.
